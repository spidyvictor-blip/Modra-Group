Good to know

Common questions.

How does billing work?

Care plans are billed monthly and you can cancel anytime. À-la-carte services are billed when delivered. We'll send a clear quote before any work begins.

Which areas do you serve?

We focus on West Michigan, with an emphasis on higher-end homes where a premium, hands-on level of care makes the biggest difference.

What do you handle — and what don't you?

We handle property care: cleaning, exterior upkeep, curb appeal, seasonal and turnover work. For specialized or licensed trade work, we coordinate trusted, licensed professionals so it's done right.

What makes Modra different?

We're veteran-owned, and we're part of a full-cycle group that also renovates and funds real estate — so we understand high-end properties from every angle.

Our funding approach

Own capital first, financing when it fits.

Modra funds its projects with the founders' own capital, and when a deal calls for more, we arrange financing through trusted, licensed brokers and lenders. Every dollar is matched to the right stage of a project, with conservative underwriting before anything moves.
